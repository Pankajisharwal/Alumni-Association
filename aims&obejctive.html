Aims and Objectives

  • To establish a strong network of alumni: The alumni association aims to create a community of alumni who can support each other professionally and personally.
  • To preserve college traditions: The alumni association helps to preserve the traditions and values of the college by organizing events that celebrate the institution's history and culture.
  • To provide career support: The alumni association helps recent graduates and current students to find jobs and build their careers by offering mentoring, job placement services, and networking opportunities.
  • To foster lifelong learning: The alumni association encourages continued education and intellectual growth by organizing educational events and providing opportunities for alumni to stay engaged with the college.
  • To enhance the college experience: The alumni association works to improve the overall college experience for current and future students by providing feedback to the administration on issues affecting the student body, and advocating for improvements.
  • To promote alumni involvement: The alumni association seeks to increase alumni involvement in the life of the college by encouraging participation in events, volunteering, and serving on advisory boards.
  • To build relationships with other alumni associations: The alumni association may seek to build relationships with other alumni associations at other colleges and universities, promoting networking opportunities and exchanging ideas and best practices.
  • To promote diversity and inclusion: The alumni association may work to promote diversity and inclusion at the college, advocating for policies and initiatives that support students, faculty, and staff from diverse backgrounds.
  • To provide social and recreational opportunities: The alumni association may organize social and recreational events to bring alumni together and foster a sense of community among graduates of the college.
