Chhaju Ram Memorial Jat College has a long tradition of pursuing excellence in teaching.The vision of the institution is to be a benchmark in education through innovation and excellence, to prepare ethical, confident and disciplined citizens and leaders of tomorrow and to produce professionals who are critical thinkers, creative planners and effective practitioners. The College has decided to create a Department of Alumni Relations so as to cherish long-lasting and meaningful with its former students for mutual pride and enrichment.

The major objectives of the Department are to:

  • Strengthening the relationship between alumni and the college. The alumni association may aim to establish and maintain a strong connection between the college and its graduates, fostering a sense of loyalty and pride among alumni.
  • Supporting the professional development of alumni: The department may provide career development resources, networking opportunities, and other services to help alumni advance in their careers.
  • Enhancing the reputation of the college: The alumni association may aim to promote the college accomplishments and achievements to potential students, donors and other stakeholders.
  • Providing opportunities for alumni to stay involved with the college: The department may organize social events, volunteer opportunities, and other activities that allow alumni to stay connected with the college and with each other.
  • Engaging young alumni: The department may focus on engaging recent graduates and helping them connect with each other and the college.
  • Providing mentorship opportunities: The department may facilitate mentorship programs that connect alumni with current students or younger alumni who are seeking guidance and support.
  • Preserving the history and traditions of the university: The department may work to preserve the history and traditions of the university, ensuring that they are passed down to future generations of alumni.
  • Building a strong alumni network: The department may work to build a strong network of alumni who can help each other professionally and personally.
